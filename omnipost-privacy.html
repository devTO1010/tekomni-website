OmniPost Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. About OmniPost

OmniPost is an AI-powered marketing platform operated by TekOmni Consulting LLC ("TekOmni," "we," "us," or "our") at omnipost.tekomni.com. OmniPost helps businesses create, schedule, and publish marketing content to social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect when you use OmniPost, how we use and protect that information, and the choices you have. This policy applies specifically to OmniPost. For our general business website privacy practices, see our main Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an OmniPost account or are invited as a client, we collect: your name, email address, password (stored as a salted hash), business name, role, and any profile details you provide.

Brand and Business Information

To generate marketing content for you, OmniPost stores brand information you supply: company description, target audience, tone of voice, brand colors, fonts, logos, reference media (product photos, team photos, etc.), website URL, and any other materials you upload.

Social Media Account Data (OAuth)

When you connect a social media account (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or TikTok) through OAuth, we receive and store:

  • An access token issued by the platform, which authorizes OmniPost to publish content and read engagement data on your behalf
  • A refresh token (where applicable) used to renew expired access tokens
  • The account identifier (e.g. Facebook Page ID, Instagram Business ID, LinkedIn member URN, TikTok Open ID)
  • Public profile metadata: account name, username, avatar URL, page category

We never receive or store your social media password. OAuth is a delegated authorization standard — you authenticate directly with the platform, and the platform issues us a token scoped to specific permissions you approve.

Content You Create

OmniPost stores all marketing content you create or generate through the platform: post text, hashtags, calls-to-action, scheduled publish times, AI prompts you submit, and any media (images, videos) you upload or generate.

Engagement Metrics from Social Platforms

After your content is published, OmniPost periodically retrieves engagement metrics from the relevant social media platform's API: likes, comments, shares, impressions, reach, and other public statistics. We use this data to show you analytics and to improve future content recommendations.

AI Interaction Data

OmniPost uses AI models to generate content. Your prompts, generated outputs, and feedback you provide (e.g. thumbs up / thumbs down) are stored to improve future suggestions for your account.

Usage and Device Data

We automatically collect technical information when you use OmniPost: IP address, browser type, operating system, page views, feature usage, error logs, and session timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the OmniPost service: generate, schedule, and publish content on your behalf
  • Authenticate you and secure your account
  • Display your content, analytics, and account state
  • Improve AI-generated content quality based on your feedback and engagement results
  • Send transactional emails (account notifications, content approval requests, weekly digests)
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information, your social media data, or your content to advertisers or data brokers.

4. How We Share Information

With Social Media Platforms

When you publish content through OmniPost, the post is sent to the social media platform you selected (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or TikTok) using the access token you authorized. The content then appears on your account on that platform under their terms of service and privacy policy.

With Service Providers

OmniPost uses third-party service providers to operate the platform. These providers receive only the data needed to perform their function and are bound by confidentiality obligations:

  • Supabase — database hosting, authentication, file storage
  • Railway — application hosting
  • Modal — serverless compute for background jobs
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Google Gemini — AI text generation
  • fal.ai — AI image generation
  • Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok APIs — content publishing and metrics retrieval

Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TekOmni, our users, or others.

Business Transfers

If TekOmni is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account information — retained until you delete your account
  • OAuth tokens — retained until the token expires, you disconnect the social account, or you delete your account, whichever is first
  • Content — retained until you delete it, or for 90 days after account deletion (in case of accidental deletion or recovery)
  • Engagement metrics — retained for the lifetime of your account for analytics
  • Audit logs — retained for 12 months for security and compliance

6. Your Rights and Choices

Disconnect a Social Media Account

You can disconnect any connected social media account at any time from Portal → Connections. When you disconnect, we immediately invalidate the stored access token. You can also revoke OmniPost's access directly from the social media platform's settings (Facebook Business Integrations, LinkedIn Permitted Services, TikTok Authorized Apps).

Delete Specific Content

You can delete individual posts, drafts, or media items from within OmniPost at any time.

Delete Your Account

To delete your OmniPost account and all associated data, email us at TellMeMore@TekOmni.com with the subject line "OmniPost Account Deletion." We will process your request within 30 days. Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, brand data, content, OAuth tokens, and engagement history.

Export Your Data

To request an export of your OmniPost data, email TellMeMore@TekOmni.com with the subject line "OmniPost Data Export." We will provide a machine-readable copy within 30 days.

Other Rights

Depending on your location (e.g. residents of California, the European Union, or other jurisdictions), you may have additional rights including the right to access, correct, restrict processing of, or object to processing of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Data Security

We protect your data using industry-standard practices:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted database storage at rest
  • Row-level security (RLS) so each customer's data is isolated
  • OAuth tokens stored separately and revocable on demand
  • Bearer token authentication on all API endpoints
  • Audit logging of sensitive operations

While we take reasonable steps to secure your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

OmniPost is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. International Users

OmniPost is operated from the United States. If you access OmniPost from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notice at least 30 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
